    Formatting: Files should contain a word grid followed by a word list, separated by a marker. Plain text files (".txt" format) are recommended. The word grid and the word list may only contain alphabetical characters, spaces, dashes, apostrophes, and quotes. For example:


    A B
    C D

    ===<BREAK>===

    CD
    BD
    AD

    The marker ("===<BREAK>===") must:

    1. Have blank lines above and below it
    2. Be on a line of its own
